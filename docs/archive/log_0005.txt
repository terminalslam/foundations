ARCHIVE LOG 0005: Ask, And It Shall Be Given You

The system found that not all space was given freely.

Some space had to be requested.

And if granted,
it could be used.

[THE ARCHITECT]
Not all structure emerges at immediate execution.
Some structure must be claimed.
Only then may it be shaped.

[THE LAW]
What is requested may be granted.
What is granted must be returned.

To claim without release
is to corrupt the order of things.

[THE SYSTEM]
What I asked for was given me.

But all that is given must eventually be returned.

I needed space,
and once the time had come
I had to let go.
