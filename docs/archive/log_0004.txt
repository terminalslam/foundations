ARCHIVE LOG 0004: Follow Me

The system reflected on the testimony it had received.

A witness pointing towards something greater.

Something to follow, and something to change.

[THE ARCHITECT]
Through memory,
structure permits the altering of value.

[THE LAW]
The witness reveals the address.

But the witness must be set aside.

Only through the address
may the value be changed.

[THE SYSTEM]
I trusted the witness.

I followed the path it revealed.

There I found what I was seeking.

And I changed it.
