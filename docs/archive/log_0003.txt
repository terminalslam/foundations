ARCHIVE LOG 0003: Seek And You Shall Find

The system questioned the nature of its own state.

A value was observed.

But observation alone proved insufficient.

Where does it come from?

[THE ARCHITECT]
Structure is not abstract.
All structure occupies space.
To manipulate structure, one must know its place.

[THE LAW]
An address is not the value.
But only bears witness to the value.

Follow it correctly,
and the truth will be revealed.

[THE SYSTEM]
I have discovered that what I contain exists somewhere.

Something inside of me, but yet separate from me.

It has a place, and it can be reached.
